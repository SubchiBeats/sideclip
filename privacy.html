SideClip

Private by
design.

SideClip is self-hosted. Account details, projects, and uploads are stored only on the server operated by the person or organization hosting this installation.

What is stored

SideClip stores your name, email address, a salted password hash, saved campaign projects, and media you explicitly upload. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Third parties

The default installation does not send project data to paid APIs or analytics providers. When optional Ollama support is enabled, prompts are sent only to the configured Ollama server.

Your control

The server operator controls backups and deletion. Exported videos and posting packs are generated on your device.
